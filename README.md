# Restaurant-Analysis-Project
![](https://github.com/onwudiweginikachukwu/Restaurant-Analysis-Project/blob/main/RA.png)


## Project Overview
This project explores restaurant data to generate actionable insights for business owners and entrepreneurs. The goal is to understand customer behavior, pricing, ratings, cuisines and service patterns that influence restaurant performance and profitability.


## Business Objectives
- Identify performance trends
- Understand customer preferences
- Evaluate pricing
- Support data-driven business decisions.


  ## Tools and Skills Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modelling
- Data Visualisation
- Dashboard Design
- Business Intelligence
- Data Analysis


## Key Insights
1. Mexican cuisine has the highest customer demand, making it the most preferred cuisine among consumers. This indicates a strong and consistent market demand for Mexican food.

2. Brewery cuisine has the highest average restaurant rating, suggesting that restaurants in this category deliver a better overall customer experience, even though they are not the most preferred cuisine.

3. Customer preferences influence restaurant success but do not solely determine ratings. While Mexican cuisine attracts the most customers, Brewery restaurants receive the highest ratings, showing that service quality and dining experience also play a major role in customer satisfaction.

4. Students aged 18–24 make up the largest customer segment, indicating that restaurants are primarily serving young adults.

5. Most customers have a medium budget, suggesting that the market is driven by consumers looking for quality meals at affordable prices.

6. Cuernavaca, Morelos has the highest concentration of customers in the dataset, making it an important market for restaurant businesses.

7. Restaurants offering valet parking received higher average ratings, suggesting that convenient parking may contribute to a better customer experience.

8. Restaurants with bar-only smoking areas recorded higher average ratings than other smoking policies in the dataset. While this does not prove that smoking policies directly improve ratings, it highlights a pattern that restaurant owners may wish to investigate further.

9. The comparison between customer demand and restaurant supply reveals differences across cuisines, indicating that some cuisine categories may present opportunities for new restaurant businesses if customer demand is stronger than the existing supply.

## Recommendations
    
- ### Focus on high-demand cuisines:
   Entrepreneurs should consider offering Mexican cuisine since it is the most preferred by customers, while also adopting the quality standards observed in highly rated Brewery restaurants.

- ### Target the right customer segment:
  Design menus, promotions, and restaurant experiences that appeal to students and young adults aged 18–24, as they make up the largest consumer group.

- ### Offer medium-priced meals:
   Since most customers fall within the medium budget category, restaurants should provide quality meals at affordable prices to attract a larger market.

- ### Choose strategic locations:
   Entrepreneurs should consider investing in locations such as Cuernavaca, Morelos, where the dataset indicates strong restaurant activity.

- ### Improve customer experience:
   Invest in features such as convenient parking, excellent customer service, and a pleasant dining environment, as these are associated with higher customer ratings.

- ### Identify market opportunities:
   Before opening a new restaurant, compare customer demand with the number of existing restaurants in each cuisine category. Cuisines with high demand but relatively low supply may present good business opportunities.

- ### Use data for continuous improvement:
   Regularly collect and analyze customer feedback to understand changing preferences and improve restaurant performance over time.
